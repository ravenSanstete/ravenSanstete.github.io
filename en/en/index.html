    About Me

    Welcome! I’m Xudong Pan (潘旭东), a researcher working on AI Safety and Security. I’m currently an Assistant Professor at School of Computer Science, Fudan University, where I earned my Bsc. and PhD degree in 2018 and 2023 (closely work with Prof. Min Yang and Mi Zhang at System Software and Security Lab on AI security research). My current research focus: Safety/Security of LLM and MLM LLM/MLM-Integrated Application Security Agent Safety and Frontier AI Safety Technically, evals, attacks and defenses…

    Research Highlights

    Safety of Large Models LLM Safety (Multiple research projects with top AI corporations) Frontier AI Safety (First empirical evidence on AI’s self-replication capability) Data Security: Private Data Reconstruction Attack [USENIX Security’22, NeurIPS’22, IEEE S&P’20 (LLM-related)] AI Model Protection (Copyright&Forensics) [USENIX Security’23, KDD’23, KDD’22, ESORICS’21] Model/Algorithm Security: DeepFake Mechanism [IEEE TPAMI’20, ICML’18] Distributed Learning [USENIX Security’20] Inference Acceleration [ACL‘23 (LLM-related)] Physical Adversarial Example [USENIX Security’23, ICDE’22, …] Stealthy Backdoor/Trojan Attack [IEEE S&P’24, USENIX Security’22, ACSAC’21, …]
