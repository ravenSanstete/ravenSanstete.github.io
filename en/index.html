About Me

Welcome! I’m Xudong Pan, a researcher working on AI Safety and Security. I’m currently an Assistant Professor at School of Computer Science, Fudan University, where I earned my Bsc. and PhD degree in 2018 and 2023 (closely work with Prof. Min Yang and Mi Zhang at System Software and Security Lab on AI security research).

My current research focus:

  • Safety/Security of LLM and MLM
  • LLM/MLM-Integrated Application Security
  • Agent Safety and Frontier AI Safety

Technically, evals, attacks and defenses…

(Updated at 4th, Feb 2025)

Research Highlights

  • Safety of Large Models

    1. LLM Safety
      • Multiple research projects with top AI corporations
      • WhitzardIndex: A Dynamic Safety Evaluation Benchmark for Chinese Commercial LLMs
    2. Frontier AI Safety
      • First empirical evidence on AI’s self-replication capability
      • Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.12140

  • ML Data Security:

    1. Privacy Attacks [USENIX Security’22, NeurIPS’22, IEEE S&P’20 (LLM)]
    2. Copyright Attacks/Protection (Copyright&Fingerprinting) [USENIX Security’23, KDD’23, KDD’22, ESORICS’21]

  • Model/Algorithm Security:

    1. DeepFake Mechanism [IEEE TPAMI’20, ICML’18]
    2. Distributed Learning [USENIX Security’20]
    3. Inference Acceleration [ACL‘23 (LLM)]
    4. Physical Adversarial Example [USENIX Security’23, ICDE’22]
    5. Stealthy Backdoor/Trojan Attack [IEEE S&P’24, USENIX Security’22 (LLM), ACSAC’21]
